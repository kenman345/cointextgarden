12th ConTeXt Meeting 2018

The 12th ConTeXt Meeting will take place in Prague–Sib≈ôina, Czech Republic

September 2–8 (Sun–Sat), 2018

You are cordially invited to join the 12th ConTeXt Meeting. The meeting will host ConTeXt and LuaTeX developers and users and gives you the opportunity to present your questions, experiences, results and ideas on ConTeXt and its future development. The talks will be followed-up by tutorials on different ConTeXt and LuaTeX techniques.

Call for submissions

The theme for this meeting is

Unusual usage of ConTeXt

As in previous years, anything at all related to ConTeXt that you would like to share is an acceptable subject for a presentation, tutorial, discussion, Q&A session, demonstration, workshop, recital, sketch, or sermon.
Get inspired by abstracts.

The programme committee is Hans Hagen and Taco Hoekwater, feel free to email them with hints and ideas.
