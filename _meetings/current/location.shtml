Location

Prague–Sibřina

The meeting will be held in Sibřina, small village next to Prague.

Accommodation

U Škodů is the renovated homestead from 1785, nice place for talks and relaxation. The owners also run a small farm producing mostly honey and herbal products.

Food

Breakfast will be provided at the farm. Lunch and dinner will be served in the neighboring restaurant.
